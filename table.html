Essential Oils

Essential oils are aromatic liquid substances that have been extracted from certain flowers, trees, grasses, roots, and leaves.
These powerful oils are the very essence of nature and have been utilized for centuries to bring healing and comfort to the physical, mental, spiritual, emotional, and environmental aspects of our lives.
